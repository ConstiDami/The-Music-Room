The Music Room

An interactive videogame to discover music instruments

Find Out More

Laboratory 2021-22

This website presents The Music Room, a project realized for the Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge Laboratory 2021-22 of the University of Bologna. It is an interactive viedogame using photogrammetry and 3D modelling to introduce some musical instruments to the user.
